About Us

Where creativity meets good company

Paint & Bubbles is Brighton's favourite creative events studio. We host relaxed, fun painting and craft sessions for all skill levels — whether you're a total beginner or a seasoned artist, you'll leave with something you're proud of and a smile on your face.

Every event includes all the materials you need, a welcoming space, and drinks to keep the creativity flowing. No experience necessary — just show up, let loose, and enjoy the ride.
